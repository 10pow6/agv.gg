Who We Are

An Earth 2 community, first and always.

AGV is a group of Earth 2 players who became friends — engineers, artists, quants, and dreamers scattered across the globe, united by one virtual planet.

We build colonies, craft tools, share intel, and help new players find their footing. No hype, no gatekeeping — just people who genuinely enjoy shaping the new earth together.

Our Codex

Three simple principles

As travelers in two worlds, this is how we carry ourselves.

Truth

We conduct ourselves honestly. We do not abuse system exploits and practice ethical disclosures. We strive for open and transparent communication.

Beauty

We respect the grid. We want to use the new world's territories as canvas for beautiful structures and places to come alive.

Goodness

We help each other. As a community, we support new players and keep a good spirit regarding the further progression of the game and community.
